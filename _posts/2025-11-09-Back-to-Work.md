## Master Application and Bachelor Thesis

One focus of this week was working on my Master application and trying to figure out what I will do as my bachelor thesis.

Regarding the master: I am planing on applying for "Sustainable Management and Technology" in Lausanne (EPFL, UNIL, IMD) and "Management, Technology and Economics" at ETH. For both I need to write a motivation letter (I will share it here once it is ready). As I am an external student for Lausanne, I need to get three people to write me recommendation letters. This is harder said than done with the nature of my bachelor lectures of having many people in a large room. This means very few lecturers know who I am and could write a letter about me.

For those interested here are the links to the two masters:
- Lausanne: [https://e4s.center/education/e4s-smt-master/](https://e4s.center/education/e4s-smt-master/)  
- Zurich: [https://mtec.ethz.ch/studies.html](https://mtec.ethz.ch/studies.html)

For the bachelor thesis I had another two calls at the beginning of the week and I will likely have another one next week. Then I have to make some tradeoffs. One possibility would be using machine learning on the noise of climate models: I would be interested from a purely technical level. Another is to do technoeconomic modelling together with social research on an energy community: This doesn't involve as much coding but is thematically better aligned with where I will likely head next. Then there are other elements of how I perceived my supervisor, how likely it is that something useful comes out of the thesis, and so on. Not easy...


## Orchestra and TwoSet Violin Concert

Wednesday and Thursday evening were very musical. First a concert, then a rehearsal.

![TwoSet]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-11-09/twoset.jpeg)

On Wednesday I went to the World Tour of TwoSet Violin. They are a duo of violinsts that started a youtube channel while still in university and are now doing live shows. It is a mix of concert and comedy show. They for example played "violin charades". Their team had prepared Toronto related words like racoon, sorry, and streetcar. One of them looked at the word and tried to communicate it to the other solely by playing the violin. It was hilarious. For people wondering here are two exerpts of past tours:

- They played the same encore in Toronto: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qYPPd7Hvnf8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qYPPd7Hvnf8)  
- The Carprice while Hola Hooping was again part of the show but this time Hilary didn't show up ;-( [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eOjO4ekcJQA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eOjO4ekcJQA)

Thursday evening it was time for another orchestra rehearsal. There were some breaks in the last few weeks because of reading week and because the conductor wasn't there at some point. Now we only have 3 more weeks until it is time for the concert. I am so used to more rehearsals and at least one or two whole days on a weekend but I guess I just have to roll with it and practice. The weird part is that the pieces are really playable for the most part and then every so often there are incredibly fast parts that I practice at home and then in the rehearsal it feels like they are as unplayable as the week before... Making music with others is so much fun though and I am excited for what is to come.

## Orchester und TwoSet-Violin-Konzert

Mittwoch- und Donnerstagabend waren ganz im Zeichen der Musik – zuerst ein Konzert, dann eine Orchesterprobe.

![TwoSet]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-11-09/twoset.jpeg)

Am Mittwoch war ich beim „World Tour“-Konzert von *TwoSet Violin*. Das sind zwei Geiger, die während ihres Studiums einen YouTube-Kanal gestartet haben und nun mit einer Mischung aus Konzert und Comedy auf Tour gehen.  
Zum Beispiel spielten sie eine Art „Geigen-Pantomime“: Einer bekam ein Wort – etwa „Racoon“, „Sorry“ oder „Streetcar“, typisch Toronto –, und musste es dem anderen nur durch Geigenspiel verständlich machen. Es war urkomisch!  
Für alle, die neugierig sind, hier zwei Ausschnitte aus früheren Tourneen:

- Sie spielten in Toronto dasselbe Encore: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qYPPd7Hvnf8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qYPPd7Hvnf8)  
- Der „Caprice mit Hula Hoop“ war wieder Teil der Show – nur Hilary war diesmal leider nicht dabei :-( [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eOjO4ekcJQA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eOjO4ekcJQA)

Am Donnerstag stand dann wieder eine Orchesterprobe an. In den letzten Wochen gab es einige Unterbrechungen – einmal wegen der Reading Week, einmal, weil der Dirigent nicht da war. Jetzt bleiben nur noch drei Wochen bis zum Konzert!  
Ich bin eigentlich gewohnt, öfter zu proben – manchmal sogar ganze Wochenenden – aber hier muss ich mich wohl einfach anpassen und selbst fleißig üben.  
Etwas kurios ist, dass die Stücke größtenteils sehr gut spielbar sind, und dann plötzlich kommen Passagen, die unglaublich schnell sind. Ich übe sie zuhause, aber in der Probe fühlen sie sich jedes Mal wieder genauso unspielbar an wie zuvor …  
Trotzdem: Musik gemeinsam zu machen macht riesig Spaß, und ich freue mich schon sehr auf das Konzert!
