##Â Visit Omi and Opi

This week I had very special guests in Toronto: Omi and Opi. It was so wonderful, and a bit strange, to see them here! After two months I have settled in. I have found a rhythmn and I know my around the places I have to go often. Still, everything is more foreign than home. Though I recognise things I know few things or people really well. Being able to FaceTime helps with that a lot but having people I know so well here was amazing. I miss you already <3

### Tuesday: CN Tower, AGO, Parcels

We met for breakfast in their hotel. The hug from both of them felt so good. Then it was time to go up the CN Tower. It was totally worth it. Having been to different places, knowing the city a bit, and running the marathon, made the view from the top exciting. I could show Omi and Opi where I've been, where I live, where I ran. Here is a picture from the tower away from the lake. The green circle is where I live:

![CN Tartu]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/CN-Tartu.png){: width="70%" }

![CN Selfie Glasfloor]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/CN-Selfie-Glasfloor.jpeg){: width="70%" }

Afterwards we walked towards the Art Gallery of Ontario. There we found something to eat and then started exploring the museum. There was way too much to see for one visit and so I will definitely go back.

![AGO Omi Arvi]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/AGO-Omi-Arvi.jpeg){: width="70%" }

![AGO Opi Arvi]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/AGO-Opi-Arvi.jpeg){: width="70%" }

In the evening then, after eating waaayyy too much for dinner (the pita bread with humus was a lot bigger than I could have imagined for a starter), we walked to the Coca-Cola Coliseum for a concert of Parcels. Joey introduced us both to them during their visit in spring. Since then I have listened to their albums many times and so when Opi realised that they were playing, I couldn't refuse. It was soooo much fun. I really liked the way they created multiple arcs with the order of tracks they played and how they connected everything with long instrumental interludes.

![Parcels Group]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/Parcels-Group.jpeg){: width="70%" }

![Parcels Stage]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/Parcels-Stage.jpeg)

At the end of this day I was so tired that I just fell into bed once I got home at half past eleven.

### Wednesday: Run with Opi, Breakfast, and Assignments...

On Wednesday then after sleeping longer I jogged down to the waterfront to go on a run with granddad. It is so cool that we can do this and this is an absolute life goal of mine: being able to run with my grandkids at age 75. After another delicious breakfast (this time pancakes with maple syrup - my first time since arriving), I walked with them to the ferry terminal from where they visited the Toronto Islands, and then headed back home to work on one of my two assignments that were due Thursday and Friday.

### Thursday: Experiences at Chineese and Italian Restaurants.

Once my lecture was over at one we met on the campus and looked for something to eat. We decided on a chineese restaurant which prooved more difficult than expected. Spring rolls with chicken inside, way to spicy - less spicy ordered - tofu for me, and way too much for Omi. At least Opi's order was a success and he was able to finish what he ordered. I had to pick out single pieces of tofu, mix them with a lot of rice, and then they were edible for me.

We then went on a walk through the university area where I lead Omi and Opi to the lecture halls and the library I usually go to. I then took them to the amazing running store that is just up the street from where I live (and got really lucky, getting a new pair of shoes - thanks so much). Finally I brought them "home" by visiting my room. 

As our time together already came to an end we wandered through Yorkville until it was time to eat. Now it was time for Italian food and it was delicious. My pasta with tomato sauce, basilikum pesto, and goat cheese was incredible. Sadly that was our final meal together. While Omi and Opi took the streetcar back to the hotel, I went to the orchestra rehearsal. We had three amazing days together which passed way too quickly.

## Escape Room with Roomies

Sunday evening my roomies and I went to do an escape room which was a lot more fun than I expected. There was a live actor playing a blind vampire which was very scary. Taking it a lot too relaxed in the beginning we really picked up the pace and effort towards the end and just barely escaped in the final seconds.

![Escape Room]({{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/2025-10-27/Escape-Room.jpeg)

## Running post Marathon

Having completed my big atheltic goal of the year (actually the second one - the half marathon was already a big achievement), I took this week very slowly to recover. I already have ideas for possible next things I want to do. I cannot wait to work it out more conretely next week, even less to think about how I will train for them, and least of all to keep stacking bricks one day at a time! Running is so cool.
